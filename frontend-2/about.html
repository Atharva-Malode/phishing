About PhishGuard

A project dedicated to protecting users from phishing attacks using advanced detection techniques.

Our Project

PhishGuard is an intelligent phishing detection tool designed to help users identify and protect themselves from phishing attacks. Whether it's suspicious links, deceptive emails, or fraudulent images containing QR codes or text, our system analyzes the content and provides instant risk assessments.

Our mission is to make cybersecurity accessible to everyone. We believe that advanced threat detection shouldn't be limited to large enterprises—every user deserves protection.

The project leverages machine learning and pattern recognition algorithms to detect phishing attempts with high accuracy, helping you stay safe online.

🔗 Link Detection

Analyzes URLs for suspicious patterns and phishing indicators

📧 Email Analysis

Identifies fraudulent email content and spoofing attempts

🖼️ Image Scanning

Detects phishing QR codes and malicious text in images
